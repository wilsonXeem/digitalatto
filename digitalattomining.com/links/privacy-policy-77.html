terms and conditions, accessible at https://digitalattomining.com/

1.1. Account Creation Information

When you sign up for the Service or create an account on the Site, we collect and use
the Personal Data you provide to us such as your name, email address, or billing
information, credit card information, cryptocurrency wallet address, IP address, or other
data that can be reasonably linked to such information by Digitalatto Mining, such as

sign up for the Service, which information will be used solely for the purpose of
providing access to your user account. Upon signing up, you will have the option of

If you are opening an account for your clients as a business user, you certify that where
you have provided information regarding them you have received prior consent to

Privacy Policy, to which they have agreed.

The Site is not directed at a person under the age of eighteen (18). In general, and with
