When speaking of Web3 coins, some may automatically think of cryptocurrency tokens such as Bitcoin and Ethereum – but it’s much more than that. In fact, Web3 coins form part of a much larger ecosystem which many believe is the future of both the internet and finance as we know it. Expanding further than just being an online currency, digital coins also give investors the power to vote on issues concerning the relevant protocols.

Starting With the Basics

Web3 (also known as Web3.0) is the next generation of the internet, an ecosystem that is decentralized in nature, integrating the power of blockchains, Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs), cryptocurrency and Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs). Although these concepts have been floating around the web for quite some time – some still find it difficult to fully understand what all of them mean. Blockchains are online ledgers that store information across a number of locations on a network, which makes hacking and data leaks a thing of the past. NFTs are one-of-a-kind, digital assets, which live on a blockchain, can be purchased and sold freely, or be held as an investment. DAOs are the next generation of organizations, which function on rules that are encoded on blockchains, ensuring full transparency and decentralized, democratic governing bodies.

 

What brings the Web3 ecosystem to life is Web3 coins, which will allow for all transactions to take place, through the use of crypto wallets. On top of this, many coins give investors voting power when it comes to important decisions and changes regarding the protocol. Seeing as so many different Web3 coins exist, some may find it confusing to decide which one/s to invest in. Whether a beginner or expert in the crypto universe, a vast number of Web3 coins exist which facilitate different uses across a number of industries, all focusing on different functions. Coins such as GAL, HNT and FIL are certainly the top Web3 coins to look out for in 2022, as they are making strides in bringing Web3 to life.

1, Project Galaxy (GAL)

Having launched at the beginning of 2022, the Project Galaxy ecosystem aims to provide users with an open, collaborative credential network. Being the largest Web3 credential data network in the world, Project Galaxy helps developers and DAOs to leverage NFTs and digital credential data. This is done through curators utilizing their native GAL token and signaling a credential data set’s value, which is then incentivized through the split of revenue amongst credential stakeholders. The main goal of the Project Galaxy team is to build better products and communities via the credential data network they’ve built.

2. Helium (HNT)

 Often being labeled as “The People’s Network”, Helium allows for low-power devices to communicate with one another and transmit data over a network of nodes, called hotspots. This decentralized network focuses on powering  IoT devices, which are physical devices with the capability of connecting to the internet, such as light bulbs and thermostats. Think smart home devices – which you control from your phone. Through the Helium network, users know that their data is less likely to get hacked. The native token used on this network is HNT, which is used for all data and transactions.

3. Filecoin (FIL)

Time and again, global corporations have failed their users through poor data protection, leading to private data being hacked, spread and misused without their consent. Filecoin is here to save the day, offering a decentralized storage solution that allows users to rent unused hard drive space through blockchain mechanisms. The native currency coin, FIL, is used to record and make transactions on the platform. Made by Protocol Labs and being built on top of InterPlanetary File System, this blockchain technology ensures top of the range, secure storage solutions.

Conclusion

Whether a newbie or a fanatic of the crypto universe, Web3 does not limit itself to one interest or industry. Through Web3 coins, not only can users explore the future of the internet, but also ensure full protection of their data, while investing in the different ecosystems that are on offer. There is certainly no better time to get involved and invest than the present.

Bitcoin Exchange Whale Ratio On Verge Of Entering “Very High Risk” Zone

The “exchange whale ratio” is an indicator that’s defined as the sum of top ten inflows to exchanges divided by the total inflows.

In simpler terms, this metric tells us what part of the total inflows are contributed by the ten largest transactions, which typically belong to the whales.

When the value of this indicator is above 0.85, it means whales occupy a very large percentage of exchange inflows right now.

As investors usually transfer their Bitcoin to exchanges for selling purposes, such a trend can be a sign that whales are dumping at the moment.

The indicator’s value usually remains above this threshold during BTC bear markets, or fake bull for mass dumping.

Zcash (ZEC) Fundamental Analysis

Zcash is a privacy-preserving cryptocurrency that transfers values anonymously and uses zero-knowledge cryptography. The protocol gives the options of shielded, transparent, and anonymous transactions to be there on the Zcash blockchain. The blockchain pays some portion of its block reward as the “Founder’s Reward” for funding the protocol development. The Founder’s Reward is currently allocated to the Electric Coin Company and the Zcash Foundation to develop the Zcash protocol, respectively.

One of the most prestigious technical teams in the world launched Zcash, and many independent organizations get funded to novelize it. How Zcash works

There are two types of transaction addresses in ZCash that are z and t addresses. The t-address are the transparent transactions that cause de-shield transactions, while the z-address represents private transactions that make shielded transactions between two users. You can get the ZEC token and trade it with other digital currencies using exchanges or brokers and then spend them or save them using only a mobile phone or a desktop and a digital wallet. In any social class, if you have an internet connection, you will have access to use Zcash as anyone else.

In Zcash, users have full control over sharing transaction’s view keys; as compared to other public ledgers, as in other public ledgers, only the people with the correct keys can see the contents of a transaction. Here, transactions are independent of the cooperation from the other parties. Researchers from various worldwide universities contributed to input in the diligent science used in Zcash. Hence, they implemented the zero-knowledge succinct non-interactive arguments of knowledge, briefly known as zk- SNARKs. This makes the cheap fees possible for private digital money.

Tokenomics

ZEC is the native currency of the Zcash network. The total supply is twenty million Zcash coins and is often referred to as lowercase zcash. The transactions of Zcash are both transparent and shielded. Transparent transactions are the ones that are the same as Bitcoin. The addresses and transaction amounts are visible in them. 

However, in shielded transactions, addresses and transaction amounts are hidden for operating. Anyone who is using shielded transactions has the ability to disclose transaction metadata selectively for auditing and regulatory purposes.

Zcash mining rewards are divided between the miners and the stakeholders, including Zcash Foundation and a few of its employees. Miners get 80 percent of the reward, while the remaining stakeholders share the remaining 20 percent. 

Zcash blockchain has been designed after Bitcoin. A similar halving procedure was adopted where rewards are halved at set intervals. In this case, the intervals would be four years apart. The next halving is approaching fast. Expected to approach in November 2022, the halving would cut the block mining rewards down to 3.125 ZEC, half of the current reward.

In 2017, Zcash improved the process by launching a second trusted setup. The improved zk-SNARKS technology facilitated more developers’ involvement. It also lowered the chances of failure of the trust set up. 

Zcash also implemented the Sapling hard fork upgrade in 2018 to improve the activity and confidentiality of the cryptocurrency’s shielded transactions. In 2021, ECC announced the plans to shift Zcash away from its proof-of-work consensus mechanism with a more interoperable and energy-efficient staking system. Zcash also decided to eliminate the trusted setup to add future upgrades.
